Blade / Projecting Signs

Wall-mounted perpendicular signage that projects outward for maximum street visibility and wayfinding.

Blade or projecting signs extend perpendicular from building facades, making them visible from multiple angles along walkways and streets. These signs are essential for retail locations, restaurants, and service businesses in high-footfall areas.

Perfect for: Retail shops, cafes, restaurants, clinics, salons, boutiques, and any business requiring strong street-level visibility.

Our Signature Finishes

[Image: Limewash]

Lime Paint (Limewash)

A breathable, mineral-based finish that creates soft, cloudy movements and a velvety matte texture. Perfect for Wabi-Sabi aesthetics.

[Image: Microcement]

Microcement

Seamless, durable, and waterproof. The ideal solution for modern bathrooms, floors, and kitchen countertops without grout lines.

[Image: Venetian Plaster]

Venetian Plaster

Polished, marble-like elegance. High-gloss or textured finishes that add depth and luxury to feature walls.

About GetNoticed Productions

GetNoticed Productions is a Singapore-based signage practice focused on one thing:
getting signage approved, built properly, and installed safely.

We work closely with retail operators, office tenants, exhibition teams and contractors who need signage done right the first time — without guesswork, last-minute surprises, or approval delays.
